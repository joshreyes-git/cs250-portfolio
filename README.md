# cs250-portfolio

1. What did you learn about Agile and Scrum while working on this project?
I learned that Agile and Scrum focus on breaking large projects into smaller, manageable tasks that can be completed in short sprints. This made the work feel less overwhelming and helped me stay organized with a clear backlog and goals. I also saw how regular reviews and retrospectives help teams reflect on what worked and what needs improvement.

2. How did the Agile/Scrum process help you manage and complete your work during the sprint?
The Scrum process helped me stay on track by giving me a structured plan for each sprint and clear priorities for what to complete first. Having defined tasks and deadlines made it easier to manage my time and avoid procrastination. The sprint review and retrospective also helped me evaluate my progress and make adjustments for future work.

3. How will you apply what you learned about Agile in future software development projects?
In future projects, I plan to use Agile principles by breaking work into smaller tasks, setting short goals, and regularly reviewing my progress. This approach will help me stay organized and continuously improve my workflow as I learn programming and build more complex applications. Overall, Agile gives me a repeatable process I can use for both school assignments and real-world software development.
